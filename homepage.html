Please follow the steps below to log your service request:

  1. Click on Open a New Ticket.
  2. Sign in using your credentials (Customers, Employees, or Admin).
  3. Select the appropriate service from the help topics.
  4. Fill in all the required details (job type, pages, copies, etc.).
  5. Click Create Ticket to submit your request.

To check your ticket status or updates, sign in and click Check Ticket Status.
